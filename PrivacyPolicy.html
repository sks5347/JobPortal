Privacy Policy

We will promote the appropriate protection of personal information.

1. Purpose of use The company shall obtain and properly use your personal information within the scope of the purposes listed below.
・ Providing information on services provided by our company and accompanying services

2. Prohibition of disclosure / provision of personal information to third parties The Company properly manages the personal information received, and shall not disclose personal information to third parties unless legally required or when:
・ There is consent of the person

4. Outsourcing The Company may disclose personal information to third parties selected under strict examination when outsourcing all or a part of the business to them.

6. Modification The company shall comply with laws and regulations regarding personal information held and review the contents of this policy as appropriate.
